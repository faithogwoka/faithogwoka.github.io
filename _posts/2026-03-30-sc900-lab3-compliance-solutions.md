## Summary of Work Done
In this lab, I explored Microsoft Compliance solutions and learned how organizations ensure data protection and meet regulatory requirements.

Key focus areas included:
- Data classification and labeling
- Information protection policies
- Compliance management tools

---

## Microsoft Compliance Tools Covered
- Microsoft Purview Compliance Manager  
- Microsoft Information Protection (MIP)  
- Data Loss Prevention (DLP)  
- Insider Risk Management  

---

## Key Takeaways
- Compliance ensures data is handled according to legal and regulatory standards  
- Data classification helps organizations protect sensitive information  
- DLP policies prevent accidental or intentional data leaks  

---

## Real-World Application
Organizations use Microsoft Compliance solutions to protect sensitive data, enforce governance policies, and ensure compliance with laws such as GDPR and internal security standards.
